Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Computer Science and a member of the Phi Lab and Insight Lab. I am presently the Teaching Assistant for Unstructured Data at Western University.

My research interests are two-fold. The first is in applying explainable AI methods with a focus on clinical decision support systems. The second is in creating visual analytics systems which allow for interactive exploration of the information provided by these methods.
